Frequently Asked Questions

Search the most common questions or browse our FAQ categories to get the answer you need without waiting.

Ordering

  • Browse the store, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout. You can pay with Mpesa, Visa, or MasterCard.

  • Once an order is placed, changes are limited. Contact our support team immediately if you need to update shipping or item details.

  • No. You can order any item from the store. Shipping fees may still apply depending on the delivery location.

Shipping

  • Delivery within Nairobi is usually within 24 hours. Outside Nairobi, delivery may take 2-4 business days depending on location.

  • Yes, we support international shipping. Contact us for shipping costs and delivery estimates outside Kenya.

  • Once your order ships, we will send tracking details via email or WhatsApp. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

Payments

  • We accept Mpesa, Visa, MasterCard, and secure card payments from major providers.

  • Yes. Payments are processed through secure gateways and your payment details are never stored on our website.

  • Refunds are issued for eligible returns and cancellations. Please review our returns policy or contact support for help.

Returns & Support

  • We accept returns within 7 days for damaged or incorrect items. The item must be in original condition with packaging.

  • Use the contact form on our Contact page or email us at info@RetroRack.com. We respond quickly during business hours.

  • If your order has not shipped yet, we may be able to update your address. Contact us immediately with your order number.
